Veranda Nederland is de specialist

With the products of EG Veranda you can create your perfect outdoor space. Verandas, louvered roofs, glass sliding walls, sun blinds and more: we produce it all in our own factory. We find innovation very important, we are continuously working on it. Sustainability also plays an important role. We use aluminium, a very sustainable material, in all our products. 99% of the aluminium we use is recycled.
